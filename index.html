ABOUT ME

I'm Akbar V N — a cybersecurity student from Kerala, India, trained as a SOC Analyst and Ethical Hacker at RedTeam Hacker Academy and Offenso Hacker Academy.

My interests span both offensive and defensive security. I focus on identifying vulnerabilities, performing penetration testing, and also analyzing security events, monitoring threats, and responding to incidents from a SOC perspective.

I actively practice through CTF challenges, security labs, and real-world projects, and build cybersecurity tools and awareness applications to demonstrate how attacks work and how systems can be defended.

Key projects: CYBERWARE.ai — an AI-powered reconnaissance and vulnerability scanning platform, and GRASS — a cybersecurity awareness app. My goal is to become a professional penetration tester and security analyst, working internationally in the cybersecurity field.
